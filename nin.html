Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services.

How long does a typical repair take?

Most repairs are completed within 1-2 hours at your location. Complex issues may take longer, which our technician will communicate to you upfront.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Telebirr, CBE Birr, Dashen Bank, and other major mobile payment platforms through our Telegram bot. Cash payments are also accepted on-site.

Is there a warranty on repairs?

Yes, all our repairs come with a 90-day warranty. If the same issue reoccurs within this period, we'll fix it at no additional cost.

How do I reschedule my appointment?

You can reschedule directly through our Telegram bot by replying to your booking confirmation message or by contacting our support team.

Do you provide replacement parts?

Yes, we use high-quality replacement parts for all repairs. You'll receive details about the parts and their warranty before installation.
